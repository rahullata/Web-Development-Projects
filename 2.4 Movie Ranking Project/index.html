The Best Movies According to Rahul

My top 3 movies of all-time.

The Shawshank Redemption (1994):

A tale of hope and friendship, this film follows a banker's journey through wrongful imprisonment and showcases the resilience of the human spirit.

Inception (2010):

Christopher Nolan's mind-bending masterpiece explores the concept of dreams within dreams, blending intricate storytelling with stunning visual effects.

The Grand Budapest Hotel (2014):

Wes Anderson's whimsical and visually captivating film takes viewers on a comedic and stylish journey through the eccentric world of a hotel concierge and his protégé.
